About

This platform is designed for everyone to come together and debate topics related to political topics. You can submit your defense about a certain topic and someone can submit their talk to defend their position. People who would like to listen in or watch, will need to make an account.There are a few rules which both speakers and audience members will have to follow. You can read about said rules under the "Code of Conduct" section.

We have smaller discussions about other topics pertaining to science that occurs daily. The larger discussions that pertain to politics occurs monthly. You can attend the daily and weekly discussions for free, however for the monthly political debates you will need to purchase a ticket in order to attend/watch.

By clicking the button below you can start the process in buying a ticket for the monthly debates.

Contact

If you have any follow up questions you are always allowed to contact the speaker after them having defended their positions. Please do not waste the speaker's time by asking off topic questions. Only ask questions pertaining to the topic talked about in the video the speaker has uploaded.

Code of Conduct

The button below will lead you to the site containing the rules.Please be advised that if you break any of the following rules you may be banned from the site. For any offense you commit it will start at a one day ban, your next offense will be a one week ban, and then for the third offense you commit you will be banned indefinitely.

When/Where?

When purchasing a ticket, the same site will lead you to another site where the debates will be held. Debates usually start at 9am and ends at 3pm. The weekly and daily discussions are also located on this site. If you want to check the schedule you can do so by clicking the button below.
